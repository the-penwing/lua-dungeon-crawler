# Spells

## Core Logic
### Requirements
- **Magic Points (MP)**: Spells cannot be cast without 
expending an amount of MP, equal to the spells cost
- **MP Base**: Each player starts with a total of 20 MP.
future updates may allow for base MP to be increased
- **MP Regen**: MP regenerates at a rate of 2 MP/turn.
In future versions items may be added to speed up the regeneration
- **Cooldown**: The same spell cannot be cast twice in a row
